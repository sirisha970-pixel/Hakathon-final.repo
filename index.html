About

"Know Your Rights Buddy" is designed to help individuals understand their fundamental rights and freedoms. Awareness is the first step towards empowerment and justice. Know Your Rights Buddy is a friendly guide designed to help everyone—especially young people—understand their basic legal rights in a simple, relatable way. Instead of complicated law books, it gives you clear, everyday examples of how your rights protect you in school, at work, online, and in public. Think of it as your pocket-buddy who always has your back—reminding you what you can do, what you don’t have to do, and how to stand up for yourself with confidence.
